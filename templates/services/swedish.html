THE METHODOLOGY

The art of
unplugging.

Swedish massage is the foundation of modern bodywork. It uses five specific stroke styles—long gliding, kneading, rhythmic tapping, friction, and vibration—to manipulate the soft tissues for total systemic relief.

Effleurage (The Glide)

Continuous, long-flowing strokes that follow the contours of your body. This technique warms the muscles and initiates the relaxation response in the brain.

Petrissage (The Knead)

A gentle lifting and squeezing of the muscles. This increases local circulation and helps the body process metabolic waste without the discomfort of deep pressure.

Perfect for: Stress, Anxiety, & General Fatigue
